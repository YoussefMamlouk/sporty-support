Account Deletion & Data Retention

How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently delete your Sporty account, please follow these steps:

  1. Open the Sporty app and navigate to the Settings page.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the page and tap on Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your choice by typing DELETE in the confirmation box.
  4. Tap Confirm to submit your account deletion request.

Once submitted, your request will be processed, and your account will be permanently removed.

Data Deletion

When your account is deleted, we remove the following data from our systems:

  • Your account profile information (name, email, phone number).
  • Your booking history and related details.
  • Payment information (we do not store full credit/debit card details).
  • Preferences and personalized settings stored in your account.

Data Retention

Some information may be retained for legal, regulatory, or business purposes. This includes:

  • Transactional records required for accounting or taxation purposes – retained for up to 5 years in compliance with Tunisian law.
  • Aggregated, anonymized analytics data for improving our service – retained indefinitely but cannot be linked to your identity.

Additional Notes

After account deletion:

  • You will no longer be able to access any previous bookings or account-related content.
  • Deleted data cannot be recovered, so please ensure you export any information you want to keep before deletion.
